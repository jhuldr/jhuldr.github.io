My name is Peirong Liu (刘沛榕), since 2025 Fall, I have been an Assistant Professor of Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) at the Johns Hopkins University, affiliated with the Data Science and AI Institute.

I received my PhD in Computer Science from UNC-Chapel Hill in 2023, where I was beyond fortunate to be advised by Dr. Marc Niethammer. During my PhD, I spent two summers as a research intern at Meta AI in New York City. After graduation, I completed my postdoc training at Harvard Medical School & Massachusetts General Hospital. I was recognized as a Rising Star in EECS by MIT, and a Rising Star in Data Science by UCSD, UChicago and Stanford.

My research interests lie in AI for Healthcare, with a focus on Generative AI, Computer Vision, and Medical Image Analysis. For more information, please check out our group research topics.
